shapez.io shape patch finder




Type red, green, or blue to search for that color.
Type a shape code such as RRWW or RuRuWuWu to search for that shape.
Then upload your save data or manually type your game's map seed, and press search to search for it.
To get your save data launch shapez.io, click on your save file, and click the "download" icon to export it.
You can also find it in ~/.local/share/shapez.io/saves (Linux) or %APPDATA%\shapez.io\saves (Windows)
Hint: You can press F4 twice to see the coordinates in the bottom right.
No data is sent to servers, the script is done locally.
